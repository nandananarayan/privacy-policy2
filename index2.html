ACCOUNT DELETION

You can request to delete your Probatomic Account anytime. Before you proceed with the account deletion process, please read and fully understand the terms related to account deletion. If you do not agree with the terms, please stop the deletion process immediately.

About deleting your Probatomic account:

1. Once you request to delete your Probatomic account, you will enter a 30-day cool-off period during which we will preserve your data but it will not be visible to other users. You can retrieve your account anytime by logging back into Probatomic within the cool-off period. 2. If an account is permanently deleted after the cool-off period, such deletion is irreversible, and you will not regain access. All third-party accounts connected to this deleted account will be automatically disconnected, and you will not be able to log in with these third-party accounts. 3. Upon deletion, you will lose access to all content, including information, data, and records associated with your account. You will lose access to all subscriptions and or/content you purchased with that account. You will not be able to recover or request to retrieve any account-related content or information, even if you register and use Probatomic again with the same user name or email address. 4. Some content, such as comments or posts you made in the community, may remain visible to other users after your account deletion, but this information will be displayed anonymously. 5. Once you agree to delete, the relevant terms of service and other rights with Probatomic will terminate accordingly, except for those that have been agreed to continue in effective or as otherwise prescribed by laws and regulations. You will also no longer enjoy the user rights outlined in the "Privacy Policy," except as otherwise mandated by laws and regulations or required by regulatory authorities.

Before Requesting Deletion of Your Probatomic Account:

Ensure that the account meets the following conditions: • It is registered through our official channels, complies with the "Probatomic Terms of Service," and is registered by you; • There has been no abnormal activity in the last 3 months; • There are no unused virtual assets, including: ◦ No incomplete orders in the Probatomic Store; ◦ No active Probatomic Premium subscriptions; ◦ No unused credits; ◦ No purchased explore cards. • If there is an active subscription, please cancel it.

Process for Deleting a Probatomic Account:

1. Initiate your account deletion request at https://probatomic.com/profile/account/. 2. Read and agree to the Account Deletion terms. 3. Submit your account deletion request. 4. Enter a 30-day cool-off period. 5. After 30 days, your account will be permanently deleted, and you will lose all content and information associated with that account. If we have reasonable grounds to suspect or discover that your account is engaged in activities violating our Terms of Service, Privacy Policies, or any applicable regulations, we reserve the right to refuse your deletion request, halt the account deletion process, or prohibit the registration of a new user account with the same email associated with your account.

Account Deletion Restriction Notice:

To uphold the security of your account and protect your rights of property, we cannot process with account deletion requests if any of the following conditions apply: • You currently maintain an active premium membership status. • Your account has a credit balance greater than zero. • There are active, processing orders in your store account. • Your account includes any purchased courses or digital assets. If you wish to proceed with account deletion under these circumstances, please contact our support team for assistance.
